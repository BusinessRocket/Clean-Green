Why Your Home Deserves the Best Clean

A clean home isn’t just about aesthetics. Here’s why keeping your home spotless matters:

  • Boost Your Mood: Studies show that a clean environment can significantly reduce stress and anxiety. Coming home to a neat, organized space feels like a mini-escape from the chaos of the world.
  • Stay Healthy: Germs, allergens, and dust mites love unclean spaces. Regular cleaning keeps these at bay, so you can breathe easy knowing your family is protected.
  • Extend the Life of Your Belongings: From your furniture to your floors, proper cleaning and care can keep them looking brand new for longer, saving you money in the long run.

OUR REGULAR HOUSE CLEANING CHECKLIST

BATHROOM CLEANING

  • Clean the bathroom mirrors with Windex
  • Shine chrome spouts and handles
  • Clean the toilet, toilet seat, and the bowl
  • Clean the shower and sinks
  • Vacuum and mop the floors

KITCHEN CLEANING

  • Wipe down countertops and cabinets
  • Clean the exterior of the oven and fridge
  • Clean microwave inside and out
  • Vacuum and mop the floors

LIVING ROOM CLEANING

  • Dust all surfaces, including shelves
  • Vacuum and mop the floors
  • Wipe down furniture

BEDROOM CLEANING

  • Change linens on all beds
  • Dust surfaces, including furniture
  • Vacuum and mop the floors
